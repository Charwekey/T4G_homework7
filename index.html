The Wisdom of Choices: A Journey Through Tech4Girls

In a bustling digital kingdom known as Tech4Girls, a young scholar named Sam was eager to master the ancient art of logical thinking. It was the day of Lesson 12, a highly anticipated milestone on the path to becoming a coding master. The academy’s incredible instructor, Miss Epledia, gathered her eager scholars beneath the sprawling branches of the Knowledge Tree. “Today,” she announced, her eyes twinkling, “we will explore the secrets of making decisions. You’ll uncover the power of logical operators, the paths of control flow diagrams, and the subtle art of the if-else decision tree.”
